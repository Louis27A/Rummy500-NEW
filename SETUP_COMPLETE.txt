🔑 MOST IMPORTANT METHOD:

⭐ decide_insert_card(plays_in_table)

This is THE MOST CRITICAL method. Without it, the bot will always lose!

After the bot makes its initial play (bajada), it must insert cards into
existing plays to empty its hand. This method:
  • Validates insertions don't break rules
  • Prioritizes high-point cards first
  • Reduces remaining hand points optimally
  • Is called during each turn (after bajada)

❓ WHY CARD INSERTION MATTERS:

1. It's the only way to win (empty your hand)
2. It directly reduces point count
3. It enables progressive game strategy
4. Without it, the bot cannot compete
